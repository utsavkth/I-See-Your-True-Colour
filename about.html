This is not a love-dovey story. This is the real, raw, chaotic, beautiful journey of someone trying to build something that matters.

🏗️ I Built This Place

Where I work, I was there from the very beginning — pre-opening. It's like I helped lay the first bricks, built the walls, infused it with life. People came and went. That's normal. What wasn’t normal was being forgotten — being unseen, even by the very place you helped build.

At a staff party, the theme was "Dress as something starting with W." Classic me — I went full on WEREWOLF. 🐺

Surprisingly, everyone kept asking: > "Who's the wolf?"

When I finally unmasked — > "Oh... it's you." 😶

In my head, I was full Breaking Bad mode: > "I BUILT THIS PLACE. SAY MY NAME!" 🌋

But nobody did. And something cracked inside me that day.

🤯 Trying to Lift Everyone

I was the supervisor. My team? Mostly Nepalese like me. Some of them, I saw pieces of myself in. Others? Complete idiots. 😅 (Just being honest.)

I used to joke they must have left their brains at home before coming to work. I pushed them hard — to speak English, to communicate better, to rise.

During slow times, I was friendly. During busy times — volcano mode erupted. 🌋

One tiny mistake, and boom — explosion. But like a volcano, I cooled down just as quickly.

We even had a toolbox talk about communicating in English. Guess what? Nobody signed it. I got furious. Long story short — some of them complained about me.

Their words: > "Dominant." > "Dictatorship." > "Split personality."

They weren't entirely wrong either. 🎭

🍻 Drunken Destiny: The Book That Found Me

I needed answers. I went to talk with my old friend Sandeep. Funny story — we ended up drinking. 🍻

The next morning... a package at my door. Inside — > **_Surrounded by Idiots._** 🤣

To this day, I don't even remember ordering it. Drunken destiny, I guess.

I opened it. I started reading. And suddenly — dots started connecting everywhere. 🔗

🧠 Remembering The Clues

During my Professional Year Program, our facilitator Jeevan made us go to a website — Workforce of the Future — to find out what kind of employees we were: Red 🔥, Green 🌿, Yellow ☀️, Blue 🌊.

Everyone else laughed it off. Me? I bookmarked it. Without knowing that one day, this would become a core part of my understanding of people.

Surrounded by Idiots wasn't new knowledge for me — it was a forgotten truth finally waking up again.

But some people are just... built different.

Today, my dream is to build a Psychological Kundali — not mapping your stars, but mapping your instincts. Your Red, Yellow, Green, Blue energies.

Where spirituality and psychology dance together. 🌌

Foolish by design. Brilliant by instinct. Watch like a director. Strike like a pirate. Legend in the making. 🏴‍☠️🎬

🌌 Walking In Darkness

I believe in this deeply:

“I could walk in the dark —
I may stumble on pits and fall —
and I will get where I want to go.
But if someone shows the light,
it won’t be that slow,
and I might not fall as much.”

That's why I build what I build. To leave behind a few lights for those still walking in the dark.

And if I can, I will always show the light — so someone else doesn't have to stumble the way I did.

Truth is — most of the time, I'm still walking in the dark myself. 🌌
