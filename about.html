About This Project 🌟

I See Your True Colour Shining Through was born from a simple but powerful idea: What if discovering your personality wasn't about rigid labels or cold tests, but about real-life moments? Moments that reveal your instincts when things get messy, exciting, unpredictable.

Inspired by Thomas Erikson’s Surrounded by Idiots, this reflection tool invites you to explore your natural behavioural colour — not by answering boring theoretical questions, but by stepping into relatable life scenarios.

Who is this for?

  • 🌈 Anyone curious about their emotional instincts
  • 🌈 Anyone who wants to understand themselves or others better
  • 🌈 Anyone tired of boring, clinical quizzes

We believe growth starts with self-awareness — and self-awareness starts with honest reflection.

You're not just picking answers. You're seeing yourself. And that’s powerful.
